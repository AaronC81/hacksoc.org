Introduction to Category Theory

This course has finished.

Location and Times

G/001 at 20:15 every Thursday night in Weeks 1, 2, and 3.

Prerequisites

Familiarity with Haskell syntax and the concept of a typeclass will be useful, but will be introduced if people don't already know them.

Course Content

  • Categories, functors, and natural transformations
  • Categories in programming languages
  • Programming with monads

Firstly, we shall have a very theoretical introduction to the concepts of category theory, and then the remaining lectures shall be a more operationally-motivated approach on how category theory is useful in programming.
