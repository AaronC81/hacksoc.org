HackSoc is a society filled with technologically-minded people, and so it's only natural that we get requests to forward advertisements to the society members. However, we recognise that not everyone is interested in this, and so we instead provide a separate mailing list, hacksoc-jobs, for this purpose. Anyone can subscribe to the mailing list, but in order to send a message to it, you must contact the committee.

Members

Want to receive job or project offers? Then sign up to the hacksoc-jobs mailing list! You can email hacksoc-jobs-list+subscribe@yusu.org, or sign up online.

Advertisers

Are you a business trying to recruit graduating students? Or perhaps just a student with a new idea. If you think you have a task which members of HackSoc would be suited for, contact hack@yusu.org with your advertisement, and we shall forward it to the hacksoc-jobs mailing list.
